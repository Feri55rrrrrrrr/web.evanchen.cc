General Information

The name of the ELMO changes each year. Here are some more recent names:
  • Ego Loss May Occur
  • Everybody Lives at Most Once
  • Every Little Mistake => 0
  • English Language Master's Open
  • Exceedingly Luck-Based Math Olympiad
  • Entirely Legitimate (Junior) Math Olympiad
  • Eric Larsen Math Olympiad
  • Ex-experimental Math Olympiad
  • Easy Little Math Olympiad
  • Extremely Last-Minute Olympiad
  • e^log Math Olympiad
  • End Letter Missing
  • Exceedingly Loquacious Math Olympiad
The ELSMO is a new recent addition to the ELMO given to the misguided souls who unfortunately pronounce MOP as "MOSP", with an additional "S". The ELSMO contestants are given three additional problems each day, which are usually ignored by most contestants. The name and appearance of the ELSMO also differ from the corresponding ELMO.
